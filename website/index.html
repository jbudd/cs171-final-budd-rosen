VC Showdown

Johnathan Budd | Jared Rosen

We have created an interactive visualization to show how venture capital firms work together and how they invest geographically, categorically, and over time. We gathered data on fifty of the top venture capital firms, from which we created four visualizations. A co-occurrence matrix shows how often two firms have invested together and what type of investment they made. A bar graph displays the number of investments each firm makes in categories ranging from biotech to clean education, a heat map displays the geographic distribution of investments across the country, and a line graph tracks the distribution of investments over time.
